Review - Programming Training Resources

2016-05-12
programming, reviews

1. Pluralsight (www.pluralsight.com)

Video training. In-depth Tech training - $29 per month (basic) , $50 per month (premium)

If you can afford just one paid training resource. Go for Pluralsight. They have an excellent collection. Video content and quality is top rate for most courses.

2. FrontEnd Masters (www.frontendmasters.com)

Most of the courses are top quality recordings of live workshops - $39 per month

The format is great. They conduct workshops with industry leaders and then record the session. It gives the feel of a live one-one training session. Also, the QA sessions are really good at the end of every section. They are still growing and need to add a few helpful features like bookmarking, view history etc.

3. Virtual Pair Programmers (www.virtualpairprogrammers.com)

Video training. Focused on Java and Enterprise java - $35 per month

Hands down best training for Java, Enterprise Java and Spring. I have been a member for over 3 years now.

4. Evented Mind(www.eventedmind.com)

Video training - $29 per month

It has nice little videos on topics raging from how web works to Metoer JS. Its owned by Chris Mather, who devloped the Router package for Meteor

5. Code School(www.codeschool.com)

Video training with interactive learning - $29 per month

Incredibly high production value. Every topic has a theme. The exercises are interactive and help understand the concepts better. The content doesn’t have a lot of depth though, I usually use codeschool to get familiar with a new technology and then go to other sites to get a better, deeper understanding.

5. Treehouse(www.teamtreehouse.com)

Video training with interactive learning - $25 per month (basic) , $49 per month (premium)

I took a trial a couple of years back. Back then they just had Ruby courses. Found it similar to codeschool.

6. Tutsplus(www.tutsplus.com)

Small how-to style tutorials and courses - $15 per month

I havent tried the paid version. They have a ton of smaller reference like tutorials + eBooks.

7. One Month(www.teamtreehouse.com)

Video based tutorials. Marketing Mantra: “Online School for Entrepreneurs” - $49 per month (basic) , $99 per month (premium)

8. Lara Casts(www.laracasts.com)

Video training . Marketing Mantra : “Its kinda like netflix for your career” - $9 per month

9. Code for Startup(www.code4startup.com)

Learn to code by building projects which are clones of popular websites - $39 per month (basic) , $79 per month (premium)

10. I Love coding(www.ilovecoding.org)

Learn via Video Screencast - $9 per month
